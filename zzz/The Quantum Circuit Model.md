**4.1. The Qubit**

- Physical vs. Logical Qubits
    
- **The Bloch Sphere:** Visualizing a qubit as a vector on a sphere
    
- Measurement bases (Z-basis vs. X-basis)
    

**4.2. Single-Qubit Gates (The "Tools")**

- **Pauli Gates:** $X$ (Bit flip/NOT), $Y$, $Z$ (Phase flip)
    
- **Hadamard Gate ($H$):** The "Superposition Creator" (Crucial!)
    
- **Phase Gates:** $S$ gate, $T$ gate ($\pi/8$)
    
- **Rotation Gates:** $Rx(\theta), Ry(\theta), Rz(\theta)$
    

**4.3. Multi-Qubit Gates (Interaction)**

- **CNOT (Controlled-NOT):** The quantum "If-Then" statement (creates entanglement)
    
- **SWAP Gate:** Exchanging states
    
- **Toffoli Gate (CCNOT):** A reversible AND gate
    
- **Universality:** Which set of gates can build _any_ circuit? (e.g., Clifford + T group)
