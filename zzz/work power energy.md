

**1. Energy** is **what you have** (The stored ability to do stuff).

- **Analogy:** The **Money** in your pocket.

**2. Work** is **what you do** (Using energy to move something).

- **Analogy:** The act of **Spending** that money.

**3. Power** is **how fast you do it** (The speed of the work).

- **Analogy:** Spending $100 in **one second** (High Power) vs. spending it over **one year** (Low Power).
